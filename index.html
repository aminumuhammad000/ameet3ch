Everything you need to know about our services, methodologies, and scaling process.

What services does Amee Technology Ltd actually offer?

We provide a premium suite of digital services designed for scale and impact:

  • Custom Software & App Development
  • AI Systems & Business Workflow Automation
  • Digital Portals (E-learning, POS, VTU, Billing)
  • Professional Branding & UI/UX Design
  • Commercial Videography & Social Media Management
How long does a custom software project typically take?

Timelines depend strictly on project scope. Simple landing pages usually take 1-2 weeks. Medium projects like e-commerce or CRM portals range from 4-8 weeks. Custom enterprise platforms with AI integrations can take 8+ weeks.

What technologies do you use in your development stack?

We utilize industry-level tools optimized for speed and security:

  • Frontend: React, Next.js, Flutter, modern HTML/CSS
  • Backend: Node.js, Express, PHP, Laravel
  • Databases: PostgreSQL, MongoDB, MySQL, Firebase
Can you modernize our existing database or legacy software?

Yes. We offer complete tech stack modernizations, legacy software refactoring, design system refreshes, seamless database migrations, and 3rd-party API integrations.

Do you provide post-launch support and training?

Absolutely. Every product we launch includes comprehensive client onboarding, user training documentation, and scalable post-launch technical maintenance to guarantee stability.
